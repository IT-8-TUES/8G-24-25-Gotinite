Cats & Sleep Cats sleep ~70% of their lives to conserve energy.
Cat Sounds Cats can produce more than 100 different sounds!
Super Hearing Cats can hear ultrasonic sounds that humans cannot detect.
Smell A cat’s sense of smell is 14 times stronger than a human’s.
Kneading Cats knead with their paws when they feel happy or relaxed.
Night Vision Cats can see in very low light thanks to a layer of cells in their eyes.
Grooming Cats spend about 50% of their waking hours grooming themselves.
Worship Cats were worshipped in Ancient Egypt and often mummified.
Long Life The oldest cat lived to be 38 years old!
Unique Whiskers Every cat has a unique set of whiskers, like fingerprints.
